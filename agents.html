Michael Freeman

Michael Freeman epitomizes integrity, hard work, energy and dedication in every detail of his real estate transactions. Mike grew up all over the world as one of four kids in a military family. His dad is a retired Marine and that led him to join and serve four years for the USMC.

Mike has spend the past 13 years protecting and serving as a police officer in the metro Atlanta area. Mike has a passion for creativity and design. He sees the possibilities and options in all his real estate transactions. Mike is a firm believer in giving back, and as a “HERO” himself, is very excited about being able to offer the Homes for Heroes program to his clients.
